About the Service

What is a Google 360° Virtual Tour?

A Google 360 Virtual Tour is an interactive, immersive walkthrough of your business — integrated directly into your Google Maps listing and Google Business Profile.

Customers can explore your space in full 360°, navigate through different areas, and experience your interiors as if they were physically present — from any device, anywhere.

  • Explore your space in 360° — room by room
  • Published directly on Google Maps
  • Integrated with your Google Business Profile
  • Viewable on mobile, desktop & tablet
  • Embeddable on your own website

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Google 360 & Customized virtual tours

What is a Google 360 virtual tour?+
It is an interactive virtual walkthrough of your business, integrated directly into Google Maps and your Google Business Profile. Users can explore your space in 360° as if they are physically present.
How much does a virtual tour cost?+
Our Google 360 Virtual Tour starts from ₹5,000 and the Customized 360 Virtual Tour starts from ₹10,000. Pricing may vary based on space size and requirements. Contact us for a free custom quote.
How long does it take to go live?+
Typically within 7 days from the photoshoot to publishing on Google Maps and Street View.
Does a virtual tour help with SEO?+
Yes. Virtual tours increase user engagement and time spent on your Google listing — both of which positively impact your local SEO ranking on Google Maps and Search.
Can I use the virtual tour on my website?+
Yes, your 360 virtual tour can be embedded easily on any website using an iframe, and can also be shared on social media platforms.
Is it a one-time cost?+
The standard Google 360 Virtual Tour is a one-time investment with no recurring charges. The Customized 360 Virtual Tour has recurring charges for hosted interactive features like hotspots and navigation.
What is a Customized 360 Virtual Tour and how is it different?Customized Tour+
A Customized 360 Virtual Tour goes beyond a standard Google tour by adding your own brand logo, a smart navigation system for seamless movement inside the tour, and Interactive Hotspots.
What exactly are Interactive Hotspots in a Customized Tour?Customized Tour+
Interactive Hotspots are clickable markers you can place anywhere inside your virtual tour. When a visitor clicks them, a popup appears showing text info, an image, a video, or a clickable link.
